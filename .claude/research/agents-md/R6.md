> Scope: Cross-repo detection of dead files, legacy patterns, and untracked artifacts.
> Methods: `git log`, cross-grep imports/references, Makefile analysis, git-status checks.

---

## Legend

| Class | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **CONFIRMED dead** | No imports, no references, old mtime, clearly replaced or abandoned. |
| **SUSPECTED dead** | No obvious references but might be used indirectly (CLI entrypoint, dynamic import, Makefile target not greppable). |
| **OUTDATED-but-might-revive** | Old but part of a process that could restart. |
