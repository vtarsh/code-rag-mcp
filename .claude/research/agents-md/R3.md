Backlinks use the `[[Relative/Path.md]]` wikilink convention (Obsidian-style) as specified by R7.

- **`gotchas/_index.md`** links to every gotcha file (cross-cutting and per-provider) and points to `../references/`, `../dictionary/`, and `../notes/_moc/`.
- **`references/_index.md`** links to all reference categories and to `../notes/_moc/` files.
- **MOC files** (e.g., `apm-MOC.md`) contain extensive reverse indexes linking to gotchas, references, and other MOCs.

- **`dictionary/_index.md`** and `README.md` explain bidirectional wiring between dictionary entries and gotchas.
